Overview

CreatorMind by ToolVora ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you visit or use CreatorMind.

By using CreatorMind, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

Short version: CreatorMind runs almost entirely in your browser. Your API key is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate and improve the platform. This includes:

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns (collected anonymously via analytics).
  • Device and browser info — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language preferences.
  • Error logs — technical errors and crash reports to help us fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Voluntarily submitted info — if you contact us via email or a form, we retain the content of that communication.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

API Keys

🔒 Your API key never leaves your browser. It is stored using your browser's localStorage and is only used to make requests directly from your device to the Groq API. We have no access to it.

CreatorMind requires a Groq API key to generate content. When you save your API key:

  • It is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage.
  • It is never sent to CreatorMind servers or ToolVora infrastructure.
  • It is used solely to authenticate your requests to the Groq API on your behalf.
  • Clearing your browser data will permanently remove it.

We strongly recommend keeping your Groq API key private and rotating it periodically through your Groq console.

How We Use Your Data

The limited data we collect is used to:

  • Understand how creators use CreatorMind so we can improve features.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • Measure the performance and reliability of the platform.
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving CreatorMind.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., "X users generated scripts this month") for general reporting purposes — these contain no personally identifiable information.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party tools that help us operate the platform (analytics, hosting), bound by strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — if required by applicable law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers — in the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, user data would transfer to the successor entity under the same privacy commitments.

Cookies & Tracking

CreatorMind uses a minimal set of cookies and local storage:

  • Theme preference — remembers whether you prefer dark or light mode.
  • API key storage — stores your Groq API key locally so you don't have to re-enter it.
  • Analytics — anonymous usage data to help us understand how the platform is used.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or fingerprinting technologies. You can clear all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage and cookies for this site.

Data Storage & Security

Content generated using CreatorMind is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers. Your generated scripts, captions, and other content exist only in your browser session.

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device and browser environment.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data.
  • Portability — request a portable copy of your data.

Since most data in CreatorMind is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly by clearing your browser storage. For other requests, contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

CreatorMind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of CreatorMind after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
